For successful investment, many investors are interested in knowing about the future situation of the market. Effective prediction systems indirectly help traders by providing supportive information such as the future market direction. As traders doesn’t have technical skills to analyze and forecast data. Stock market prediction is the act of trying to determine the future value of a company stock traded on an exchange. The successful prediction of a stock’s future price could yield significant profit. There are a lot of complicated financial indicators and the fluctuation of the stock market is very violent. Stock market predictor will help to make a better prediction based on the analysis of provided datasets and reports. Our goal is not to predict accurate stock prices of tomorrow or the day after tomorrow, Our objective is to analyze historical data of a company and find the pattern in historical data and how that pattern will flow in future. Our objective is to provide graphical view of market movements based on historical data, so traders can understand market condition will go around. Our main objective is to provide a platform for trader so that they don’t have to buy stock by guessing or blindly believing in faith.
